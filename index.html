Welcome to

The Registry of Securing Interests in Movable Property

The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia's electronic collateral registry.

The RSIMP provides the convenient services of searching, registering, and managing notices of security interest. Please see below for quick access to the main features. For more assistance, please view the Help section or the How To Guides accessible in the top navigation menu.

Create an Account

Accounts are free, and are required before registering notices of security interest into the RSIMP and to perform a certified search.

Create

Search the Registry

Types of searches include: security provider, vehicle registration number, notice registration number, and verification of a certified search.
