The Registry of Securing Interests in Movable Property

Saudi Arabia's online site for searching and filing notices of pledge in movable property


This website is where you find the Registry of Securing Interests in Movable Property (RSIMP). It is where you will register notices of security interest in movable property (Collateral), and search for existing notices of security interests. In order to register notices or obtain certified search results, you must first establish an online account with the Registry of Securing Interests in Movable Property. After submitting the account application, you will receive an email to verify and activate the account.

Public Search

Anyone can search the registry to verify whether there are any pledges on movable property. Public searches are free of charge.
