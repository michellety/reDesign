The Registry of Rights on Movable Assets

Saudi Arabia's online site for searching and filing notices of pledge in movable property


This website is where you find the Registry of Rights on Movable Assets. It is where you will register notices of security interest in movable property (Collateral), and search for existing notices of security interests. In order to register notices or obtain certified search results, you must first establish an online account with the Registry of Rights on Movable Assets.

Create an Account

In order to register notices or obtain certified search results, you must first establish an online account with the Registry of Rights on Movable Assets.

Public Search

Anyone can search the registry to verify whether there are any pledges on movable property. Public searches are free of charge.

Contact Support

If you have questions or need help with navigating the website, you may contact the Tech Support Desk. The Tech Support Desk will review your query and respond within a short time.
